Our Services

Comprehensive benefits navigation to help you and your family access SSI, SSDI, retirement, disability, and healthcare programs with confidence.

What We Offer

Full Range of Benefits Support

From initial applications to appeals, we guide you through every step of the benefits process with personalized, multilingual support.

SSI Assistance

Expert guidance through the Supplemental Security Income application process, including eligibility assessment and documentation preparation.

SSDI Applications

Comprehensive support for Social Security Disability Insurance claims, from initial filing to approval, ensuring your work history is accurately represented.

Retirement Benefits

Strategic planning for Social Security retirement benefits, helping you understand when and how to file for maximum benefit amounts.

Disability Navigation

End-to-end support navigating the disability determination process, including medical documentation gathering and examiner communication.

Medicare & Medicaid

Guidance through Medicare and Medicaid enrollment, plan selection, and coverage optimization to ensure you receive the healthcare benefits you qualify for.

Appeals Support

Dedicated representation for denied claims at every level of appeal, from reconsideration requests to Administrative Law Judge hearings and beyond.

Document Preparation

Professional preparation and organization of all required documentation, medical records, employment history, and supporting evidence for your benefits applications.

90
Days to File Your Appeal

From the date you receive your Notice of Action (NOA), you have 90 days to request a State fair hearing. File BEFORE the effective date to keep your current hours through aid-paid-pending.

Your Rights Under IHSS

California law protects your right to appeal any IHSS decision that reduces, terminates, or denies your services. You have the right to continue receiving your current services during the appeal (aid-paid-pending), be represented at your hearing, and present evidence supporting your case.

Common Reasons for IHSS Changes

Hours Reduced After Reassessment

The county social worker reassessed your needs and reduced your authorized hours.

IHSS Services Terminated

The county determined you no longer meet eligibility requirements for IHSS.

IHSS Application Denied

Your initial application for IHSS services was denied.

Protective Supervision Denied

Your request for protective supervision hours was denied or insufficient hours were authorized.

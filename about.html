About SSDI Providers

Dedicated to helping California's diverse communities access the benefits they've earned.

Our Story

SSDI Providers was founded with a simple mission: no one should be denied the benefits they deserve because of language barriers or a confusing system. Based in California, we serve individuals and families across the state who need help navigating Social Security, disability, retirement, and healthcare benefits.

With years of experience in social services and benefits navigation, our team understands the challenges that applicants face ‚Äî denied claims, overwhelming paperwork, and a system that often feels designed to discourage. We're here to change that.

We provide personalized, one-on-one support in English, Spanish, Farsi, and Tagalog because we believe that language should never be a barrier to accessing essential services.
